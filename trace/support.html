Common questions

What do I get for free?

The free tier includes the Daily Card, the Ledger, the journal, and three deep readings.

What does Trace Plus add?

Trace Plus unlocks unlimited deep readings and the full Trace experience. It costs $9.99 per month or $49.99 per year, and the yearly plan starts with a 7-day free trial.

Which deck does Trace use?

The classic 1909 tarot of Pamela Colman Smith.

Is my data private?

Yes. Your readings and journal stay on your iPhone. There are no accounts, no data collection, and no third-party SDKs. See the Privacy Policy.

How do I cancel?

iOS Settings → your Apple Account → Subscriptions → Trace. Deleting the app does not cancel a subscription.
