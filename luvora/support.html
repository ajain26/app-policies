Frequently asked questions

How do free messages work?

Every day you get a fresh allowance of free messages, plus bonus credits from the Daily Reward on your Profile tab. Luvora Premium removes the limit entirely.

How do I manage or cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are handled by Apple. Open Settings → your name → Subscriptions on your iPhone, or use "Restore Purchases" in Luvora's Settings if Premium isn't showing after reinstalling.

How do I create my own character?

Use the Create tab: pick an avatar, describe the personality, write a greeting, and add tags. Your character appears in Discover and is stored only on your device.

How do I delete a conversation or my data?

Swipe left on a chat in the Chats tab to delete it, or use the chat menu → Clear Chat. Deleting the app removes all locally stored data.

A companion said something odd or inappropriate — what do I do?

Companions are AI and can occasionally get things wrong. Please email us a screenshot at anshul.jainsm@gmail.com so we can improve the experience. Remember that companions are fictional and not a substitute for professional advice.
