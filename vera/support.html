Common questions

Is Vera a real person?

No — she's an AI voice, generated on your device. Nothing you write is read by anyone.

How do I change when Vera messages me?

Settings → Rituals — pick your morning and evening hours, or turn messages off.

How do I correct something Vera remembers?

Settings → "What Vera holds" — edit or delete anything, or add topics she must never raise.

How do I cancel?

iOS Settings → your Apple Account → Subscriptions → Vera. Deleting the app does not cancel a subscription.
