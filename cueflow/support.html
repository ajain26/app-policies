Common questions

Does voice-follow use the internet? No. CueFlow recognizes your speech entirely on-device using Apple's Speech framework. Your voice never leaves your iPhone. If your language isn't supported on-device, the prompter falls back to fixed-speed scrolling.

Is voice-follow really free? Yes. Voice-follow, fixed-speed scroll, 3-minute takes, 3 scripts, and all persona modes are free forever. CueFlow Pro is a one-time purchase that removes those limits.

Where are my recordings saved? To your own Photos library. CueFlow never uploads or keeps a copy.

Where are my scripts stored? Only on your device. There's no account and no cloud — deleting the app deletes your scripts (export or copy anything important first).

How do I restore my purchase on a new phone? Open Settings inside CueFlow and tap Restore Purchases, or use the Restore link on the paywall. CueFlow Pro also supports Family Sharing.

Can I get a refund? Refunds for App Store purchases are issued by Apple at reportaproblem.apple.com.

Voice-follow isn't tracking well — what helps? Read at a natural pace in a quiet space, make sure mic + speech permissions are granted, and confirm your language is one of the on-device-supported ones. You can always switch to fixed-speed scrolling with one tap.
