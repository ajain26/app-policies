Common questions

Is Iris a real person?

No. Iris is an AI, and the app says so plainly. Everything runs on your device; nothing you say is read by anyone.

How do I make Iris forget something?

Settings → "What Iris knows" — swipe to delete any memory, or clear everything.

How do I cancel my subscription?

iOS Settings → your Apple Account → Subscriptions → Unknot. Deleting the app does not cancel a subscription.

Why didn't my check-in arrive?

Check that notifications are allowed for Unknot in iOS Settings. Check-ins are local notifications scheduled on your device.
