Common questions

My rising sign looks wrong.

The rising sign depends heavily on an exact birth time — even 15 minutes can change it. Check that your birth time and birth place are correct in Settings. If you don't know your birth time, AstroLife builds a solar chart: your Sun, Moon, and planets are exact, but the rising sign and houses are estimated.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed by Apple: iOS Settings → your Apple Account → Subscriptions → AstroLife. Deleting the app does not cancel a subscription.

How do I restore a purchase?

Open the paywall or Settings inside the app and tap Restore purchases, making sure you're signed into the same Apple Account you bought with.

How do I delete my data?

Settings inside AstroLife → Reset everything. All your data lives only on your device, so this (or deleting the app) removes it completely.

Why didn't I get a follow-up notification?

Follow-ups fire when a real transit reaches the point you asked about — sometimes weeks or months later. Also check that notifications are allowed in iOS Settings and that quiet hours in the app aren't covering the delivery time.
