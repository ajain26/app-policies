AstroLife

The astrology app
that remembers you.

Ask anything in your own words. Every answer is grounded in your real chart — and when the sky moves on what you asked, AstroLife tells you what changed.

What AstroLife does

  • Casts your natal chart on-device from real astronomical positions
  • The Thread — one continuous conversation with your chart that remembers what you asked
  • Follows up months later when a real transit reaches the thing you asked about
  • Daily readings, weekly & yearly forecasts, moon phases, and a transit calendar
  • Compatibility readings, messages to your future self, and a home-screen widget

Private by design — your chart and questions never leave your iPhone.
