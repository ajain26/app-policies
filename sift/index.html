Sift

Scan it. Never name it.

A document scanner that reads the page, titles it, files it, and lets you find it from your Home Screen — entirely on your iPhone.

Every other scanner runs text recognition on your page, shows you a Copy button, then saves the file as 2024-05-22 00:45:29. Sift uses what it just read to name the document for you.

What it does

  • Titles itself. Scan a receipt and it saves as "Blue Bottle Coffee — Receipt — Mar 2026", not a timestamp.
  • Files itself. Sift works out whether it's a receipt, bill, ID, contract, medical record, or tax document, and categorizes it for you.
  • Findable from your Home Screen. Swipe down, type a word from inside the document, and it's there. Every scan is indexed into Spotlight.
  • Real searchable PDFs. Exports carry an invisible text layer, so the text is selectable and searchable in Preview, Acrobat, and Files.
  • Scan from anywhere. "Scan with Sift" works from Siri, Shortcuts, the Action Button, and Control Center.
  • Nothing leaves your phone. No servers. No account. No network calls. No tracking prompt — because there's nothing to track you with.

What's free

Unlimited scanning, unlimited pages, text recognition, automatic titling, automatic filing, search, Spotlight, and PDF export — free forever, no limits, no watermark. It all runs on your device, so it costs nothing to serve, so we don't meter it.

Sift Pro adds sync & backup, expiry reminders, batch tools, text export, and password-protected PDFs. You'll only ever be asked about it after you've already exported something real.
