Sift is a document scanner. It captures pages with your camera, reads the text on them using on-device text recognition, suggests a title and category automatically, and makes them searchable — including from your Home Screen. 6. Accuracy of text recognition and automatic titling

Sift reads your documents automatically and suggests titles, categories, dates, and — if you enable reminders — expiry dates. This is done by software and it will sometimes be wrong. Recognized text may contain errors, a suggested title may misread a document, and a detected date may be the wrong one. Every suggestion is editable, and you should check anything that matters. Do not rely on Sift's recognized text, categories, or reminders as a substitute for reading the actual document, and do not rely on expiry reminders as your only safeguard against a document expiring.
